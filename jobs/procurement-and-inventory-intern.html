  • NLS Waste Services Ltd

Job Description

Are you fresh out of university, ready to dive into the world of procurement and inventory management? Or perhaps you’re a seasoned professional itching for a new challenge? Either way, NLS Waste Services Ltd has a spot for you. As a Procurement & Inventory Intern, you won't just be shuffling papers—you’ll be the engine behind our procurement operations, ensuring our shelves are stocked, our suppliers are happy, and our processes are smoother than a freshly paved road.

Your day-to-day? Expect to assist with the nitty-gritty of purchasing, maintain records like a pro, and support our team in making sure that every single item is where it needs to be, when it needs to be there. Oh, and did we mention you’ll also be building relationships with vendors and learning the art of negotiation? Because you will. It’s all part of the package.

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

  • Strong grasp of procurement and inventory principles.
  • Hands-on experience with MS Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • Interpersonal skills that make collaboration a breeze.
  • Unwavering commitment to professionalism and confidentiality.
  • A keen eagerness to learn and adapt.

Education + Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Procurement, Logistics, Supply Chain Management, Business Administration, or a related field.
  • Practical experience or internships that have given you a taste of the procurement world.
