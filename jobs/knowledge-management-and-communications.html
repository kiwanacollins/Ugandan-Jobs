Job Description

If you've been itching to put your communications prowess to good use, this is the role where your words will really matter. As the Knowledge Management & Communications Advisor for the CASCADE Project, you'll be at the helm of our communication strategies, steering the way we share knowledge both internally and externally. Picture yourself crafting narratives that not only inform but inspire. You'll be the voice that ensures our message resonates with every stakeholder, from community leaders to international partners.

In this role, your days will be a mix of content creation, data analysis, and strategic planning. You'll delve into the nitty-gritty of knowledge management systems, ensuring that every piece of information is not just stored but utilized to its full potential. If you've got a knack for turning complex data into compelling stories, this is where you can truly shine.

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

  • Exceptional communication and storytelling skills.
  • Experience with knowledge management systems.
  • Strong analytical abilities to interpret and present data effectively.
  • Proficiency in digital communication tools and platforms.
  • A collaborative mindset and the ability to work across teams.
