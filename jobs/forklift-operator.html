Forklift Operator
  • Vision Group
  • Kampala, Uganda
  • Application Deadline: 9 August 2024

Job Description

Are you someone who enjoys being at the wheel of a forklift, ensuring the smooth flow of materials? Vision Group is looking for a Forklift Operator who will play a pivotal role in transporting stock supplies efficiently and safely within our Main Stores in Kampala.

Key Responsibilities

  • Load and off-load supplies with care, maintaining the integrity of items.
  • Transfer stock accurately from the warehouse to the factory, avoiding mix-ups.
  • Perform routine maintenance on the forklift to keep operations running smoothly.
  • Document and report stock movements on the factory floor.
  • Assist in bundling Newspaper returns for sale.
  • Collaborate with the transport and commercial printing teams for stock transfers.
  • Always prioritize safety guidelines while operating the forklift.

Qualifications & Experience

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (Ordinary level Certificate).
  • A valid driving permit with classes: G, B, CM, and DL.
  • Minimum of 2 years of forklift operating experience.

Skills & Abilities

  • Proficient in forklift driving.
  • Strong safety awareness.
  • Good understanding of driving regulations.
  • Basic knowledge of vehicle mechanics.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Team-oriented and able to work under pressure.
  • Flexible and dependable.

Job Overview

  • Location: Kampala, Uganda
  • Position: Forklift Operator
  • Reports to: Senior Accountant - Stores
  • Application Deadline: 9 August 2024
