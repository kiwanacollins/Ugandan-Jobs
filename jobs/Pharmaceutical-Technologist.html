Pharmaceutical Technologist
  • Baobab Circle
  • Kampala, Uganda
  • Deadline: 6 July 2024

Job Description

Are you detail-oriented and passionate about helping others? Join our telemedicine platform as a Pharmaceutical Technologist. You'll be the vital link between our customers and their medications, ensuring the smooth and accurate processing of e-prescriptions, orders, and payments.

Responsibilities

  • Handle e-prescriptions from customers using our telemedicine platform
  • Check prescription accuracy and legal compliance
  • Create and send invoices for medication costs to customers
  • Combine orders from multiple customers for the drug distributor
  • Manage payments from customers and forward them to the distributor
  • Coordinate medication distribution with Community Health Educators and Micro-Enterprises
  • Arrange courier services for medication delivery across the country
  • Communicate with customers and distributors to resolve queries
  • Maintain detailed records of e-prescriptions, orders, invoices, and payments
  • Ensure all activities comply with pharmaceutical regulations and standards
  • Keep accurate patient records using technology for inventory and orders
  • Interact with customers, answering their questions
  • Conduct regular inventory counts, checking for expired medications
  • Prepare and dispense new and refill prescriptions
  • Repackage and label medications

Qualifications and Experience

  • Diploma in Pharmaceutical Technology or Clinical Medicine
  • Registered pharmacy technician with a valid practicing license
  • 1-2 years of experience in a busy hospital setting

Competence and Skills

  • Strong communication skills, active listening, empathy, and observance
  • Mathematical and detail-oriented abilities
  • Organizational, multitasking, and time management skills
  • Problem-solving and decision-making capabilities
  • Team-oriented with a service-focused personality
  • Highly motivated with a positive attitude
