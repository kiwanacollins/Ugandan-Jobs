Senior Systems Auditor
  • Ecobank - The Pan African Bank
  • Across Africa
  • Deadline of this Job: 19th August 2024

Job Description

Ever felt like you could spot a flaw in a system just by looking at it? Well, Ecobank is offering you the chance to put those sharp eyes to work. As a Senior Systems Auditor, you won't just be another cog in the machine—you'll be the watchdog, ensuring everything runs smoothly behind the scenes. Your job will be to dive deep into the bank's systems, identify risks before they become problems, and make sure that everything is locked down tight.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct in-depth audits of Ecobank’s IT systems and internal controls.
  • Evaluate system security and data protection protocols to safeguard against breaches.
  • Identify and address vulnerabilities within the bank's IT infrastructure.
  • Collaborate with IT teams to develop and implement corrective measures.
  • Ensure compliance with regulatory requirements and IT governance best practices.
  • Prepare comprehensive reports with actionable insights for management.
  • Stay ahead of the curve by keeping up-to-date with IT audit standards and practices.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in IT, Computer Science, Accounting, or a related field.
  • CISA certification or equivalent is a strong plus.
  • Extensive experience in IT auditing, preferably in banking or financial services.
  • Solid understanding of IT governance, risk management, and compliance frameworks.
  • Excellent analytical skills and the ability to communicate complex ideas clearly.
  • A keen eye for detail and a commitment to integrity.

Why Join Ecobank?

Picture this: A company that not only values your skills but also your vision for a better Africa. At Ecobank, you're not just an employee; you're a key player in a larger mission to drive financial inclusion and economic growth across the continent. If you're passionate about making a difference and want to grow your career in a supportive and forward-thinking environment, this is the place for you.
