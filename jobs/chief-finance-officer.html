Chief Finance Officer (CFO)
  • United Bank for Africa (UBA) Uganda
  • Uganda
  • Application Deadline: 19th August 2024

Job Overview

Are you someone who thrives on financial challenges and has a passion for steering companies toward financial success? If you're looking to step into a role that demands leadership, strategy, and financial acumen, then the Chief Finance Officer (CFO) position at United Bank for Africa (UBA) Uganda might just be your next big move.

Key Responsibilities

  • Craft and execute a financial strategy that aligns with UBA Uganda's growth objectives.
  • Lead the budgeting, forecasting, and financial planning processes with precision and foresight.
  • Ensure strict adherence to both local and international financial regulations.
  • Oversee the preparation of financial statements and reports, ensuring accuracy and timeliness.
  • Drive the finance team towards operational excellence, fostering a culture of continuous improvement.
  • Collaborate closely with senior management to guide strategic decision-making.
  • Identify and manage financial risks to safeguard the bank's financial health.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or a related field (a Master's degree or certifications like CFA, CPA, ACCA is a bonus).
  • Extensive experience in finance, particularly in senior leadership roles.
  • Deep understanding of financial regulations and standards in Uganda.
  • Proven track record of managing large-scale financial operations.
  • Strong analytical, communication, and leadership skills that inspire teams to excel.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary complemented by performance-based bonuses.
  • Comprehensive health insurance and retirement benefits.
  • Opportunities for professional development and career advancement.
  • A pivotal role in a leading financial institution in Africa.
