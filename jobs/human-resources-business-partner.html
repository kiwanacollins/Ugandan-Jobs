Human Resources Business Partner
  • Jubilee Health Insurance
  • Uganda
  • Application Deadline: 6th August 2024

Job Description

Are you an experienced HR professional looking for a new challenge? Jubilee Health Insurance is seeking a Human Resources Business Partner to develop and implement strategic human capital plans, oversee performance management, and ensure alignment with corporate governance standards. This role is ideal for someone with a strong background in HR and a commitment to excellence.

Main Responsibilities

  • Strategy:
    • Develop and implement human capital plans aligned with long-term strategic objectives.
    • Identify key positions and create succession plans for leadership and critical roles.
  • Operations:
    • Participate in annual HR strategy planning and drive implementation.
    • Execute strategic initiatives, including organization design and HR planning.
    • Present recruitment needs and communicate staff movements to the Head of HR.
  • Performance Management:
    • Oversee the Performance Management process and prepare quarterly reports.
    • Guide goal setting to ensure alignment with strategic objectives.
  • Learning and Development:
    • Develop and oversee staff induction programs.
    • Ensure learning and development activities stay within budget.
  • Corporate Governance:
    • Stay updated on industry regulations and compliance requirements.
    • Adhere to Ugandan laws, insurance industry regulations, and company policies.

Qualifications

  • Academic Qualifications:
    • Master’s degree is an advantage.
    • Bachelor’s degree in HR or related field.
    • Higher Diploma in HRM or progress in CHRP.
    • Full Member of IHRM with a valid practicing certificate.
  • Relevant Experience:
    • Minimum of eight years in a similar role, with three years in a supervisory position.
    • In-depth understanding of the insurance sector’s talent landscape and skills in talent retention and development.
