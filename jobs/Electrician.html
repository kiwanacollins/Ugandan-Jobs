Electrician
  • EnviroServ (U) Limited

  • Deadline: 10 August 2024

Job Description

Join EnviroServ (U) Limited as an Electrician, where you'll tackle troubleshooting and diagnostics with the precision of a master craftsman. Your role involves removing and installing electrical components while strictly adhering to safety protocols. If you thrive on solving electrical puzzles and maintaining a high standard of safety and quality, this is your calling.

Key Responsibilities

  • Maintain and repair electrical systems, ensuring compliance with codes and standards.
  • Conduct preventative maintenance to preempt potential issues.
  • Adhere to strict safety protocols, using appropriate tools and equipment.
  • Document all maintenance activities meticulously.
  • Collaborate with team members to complete tasks efficiently and safely.

Education + Experience

  • Diploma or certificate in Electrical Engineering.
  • At least 3 years of hands-on experience in a similar role.
  • Proficiency in reading technical drawings and using diagnostic tools.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and commitment to safety.
