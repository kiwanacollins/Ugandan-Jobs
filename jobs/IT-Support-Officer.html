IT Support Officer
  • Biyinzika Poultry International Ltd
  • Bugolobi, Uganda
  • Deadline: 12 August 2024

Job Description

Are you the go-to tech guru in your circle? Do you have a knack for keeping systems running smoothly and a passion for troubleshooting? Biyinzika Poultry International Ltd, a leading name in Uganda's poultry industry, is on the hunt for an IT Support Officer to join their dynamic team at the Bugolobi Head Office.

Your role will involve the setup and maintenance of computer hardware and networks, guiding staff through technical hiccups, and rolling out new applications. You'll be the digital lifeline for the team, ensuring that operations run without a hitch.

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities

  • Installing and configuring computer systems and applications.
  • Monitoring and maintaining networks.
  • Troubleshooting hardware and software issues.
  • Strong communication skills for assisting staff.
  • Proficient in creating user accounts and managing permissions.

Education + Experience

  • Bachelor's Degree in Information Technology or related field.
  • At least 3 years of experience in a similar role.
  • IT certifications are a plus (think CompTIA, Cisco).
  • Proven ability to manage multiple tasks efficiently.
