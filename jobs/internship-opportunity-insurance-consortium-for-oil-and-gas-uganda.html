Internship Opportunity: Insurance Consortium for Oil and Gas Uganda
  • Uganda Insurers Association
  • Buliisa, Nwoya, Pakwach, Hoima, Kikube, Masindi
  • Application Deadline: 16 August 2024

Why This Internship Could Be Your Next Big Move

Feeling the pressure to land that first big opportunity after university? Or perhaps you’re stuck in a job that’s not quite lighting your fire? Imagine stepping into an industry where growth is the norm and your contribution actually matters. The Insurance Consortium for Oil and Gas Uganda (ICOGU) is here to make that dream a reality.

This isn’t just another internship—this is your gateway to the booming oil and gas sector in Uganda, all while gaining hands-on experience in insurance, one of the most critical industries supporting this sector.

What You’ll Be Doing

Your journey begins with working closely with top-tier companies like UAP Old Mutual, Uganda Reinsurance Company, and the Uganda Insurers Association. This is where theory meets practice, and you’ll be at the forefront, learning from the best in the business.

What You Need to Apply

  • National Identification Card showing your district
  • LC1 letter from your village
  • Recommendation from the Chief Administration Officer of your district
  • Cover letter detailing your passion for the insurance industry and why you’re the right fit for this internship

Internship Overview

  • Duration: 1 month
  • Location: Buliisa, Nwoya, Pakwach, Hoima, Kikube, Masindi
  • Eligible Applicants: Final year university or technical college students from the mentioned districts
  • Start Date: After selection

About the Uganda Insurers Association

Uganda Insurers Association (UIA)

The UIA is the umbrella organization for all licensed insurance and reinsurance companies in Uganda. We’re dedicated to promoting best practices in the industry and ensuring that consumers’ interests are safeguarded at every step.

  • Email: info@uia.co.ug
  • Location: 244 Acacia Avenue Kololo, Kampala, Uganda
  • P.O. Box: 8912 Kampala
